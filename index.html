ONLINE APPLICATION PROCESS GUIDELINE

1. A New Applicant: Click on Make Application on the top right of the menu to create login details to obtain your Password.
2. Log-in with your Username as your email address and Password as sent to your email address.
3. Once loggedin do the following:
i. Click to fill Form. Make sure you fill all information requested for
ii. Click button to save form if you have filled every information.
iii. Upload your Passport Pahotograph according to the specified size, and JPG or PNG format.
iv. Select Screening Centre and Date of Screening.
4. Financial Payments
i.Take the APPLICATION ID to the bank (Wema, Diamond,GTB,First Bank SkyBank, UBA). Tell the Teller you are paying for Babcock Application Fees. The fee is ₦ 2,000.00. Service charge is ₦350.00. Total ₦2,350.00.
ii. Once payment is done, then log in to the application system and Click Submit
iii. Follow all instructions on the left menu, and be sure you print your completed Application Form.
